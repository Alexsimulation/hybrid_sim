Quick Navigation

.burn


Simulates a motor burn. Enter grain geometry and oxidizer mass flux, get the burn's thrust profile and many other parameters.

.nozzle


Simulates the exhaust flow through the nozzle. Enter nozzle geometry and chamber parameters, get the nozzle's exit pressure and many other parameters.
Tip: You can also navigate via the hybrid_sim.### bar on top, simply replace the ### with the page you want to go to and press ENTER. Per example, typing hybrid_sim.burn gets you to the .burn page, and then typing hybrid_sim.home gets you back to this page.

Hybrid Rocket Motor

Here's an generic hybrid rocket motor. You can hover on parts of the motor to get information on them.

Oxidizer tank

Contains and pressurizes the oxidizer.

Valve

Controls the oxidizer mass flow to the engine.

Injector

Vaporizes the oxidizer for better mixing.

Fuel Grain

Solid fuel, burns with the oxidizer.

Nozzle

Expands the gases to generate thrust.
